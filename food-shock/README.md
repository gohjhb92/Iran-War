## Core Thesis

The real Iran war trade is **NOT oil** — that move already happened. The delayed play is the **6-9 month transmission from energy/fertilizer shock into agricultural shortages and food inflation.**

Key transmission chain:
```
Strait of Hormuz closure
        ↓
Fertilizer feedstock (natgas) & diesel disruption
        ↓  (1-3 months)
Fertilizer producers squeezed → farmers cut inputs
        ↓  (3-6 months)
Crop yield shortfalls
        ↓  (6-9 months)
Agricultural commodity price spike
        ↓  (9-12 months)
Food inflation → EM instability → consumer spending shock
```

**Equity setup:** Benchmarks bouncing into 200DMA resistance — hard to break before midterms. Buy hedges when VIX drops back below 20.
